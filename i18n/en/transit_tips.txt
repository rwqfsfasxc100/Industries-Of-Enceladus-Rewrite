IOER_TRANSIT_TIP_1|Preprocessor units draw water out of a chunk before it meets the mineral processing unit to speed up the time it takes for the chunk to be processed.

IOER_TRANSIT_TIP_2|Preprocessors with mineral efficiency bonuses increase the amount of metal you can extract from a chunk.

IOER_TRANSIT_TIP_3|Want to use the variable processed ore storage of the Cothon-217 with another ship? Use hold conversion kits like the NDSBH.

IOER_TRANSIT_TIP_4|Need extra processed ore storage? Install a hold expansion module or a bay auxiliary storage module.

IOER_TRANSIT_TIP_5|Habitat kits can improve the crew capacity of your ship, but watch for any changes in morale created by the new conditions.
